Our Responsibilities

ENVIRONMENTAL SUSTAINABILITY- We have a Responsibility to our Planet.

ADDRESSING OUR EMISSIONS IMPACT A huge part of our sustainability reporting and accountability journey has been to calculate our Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions footprints. We successfully measured and released these baselines for FY2022 and immediately proceeded to submit ambitious emissions reduction goals to the Science-Based Targets initiative. This is just the first part of how APL Logistics is are getting aggressive with our internal and external decarbonization plans.

DIVERSITY, EQUITY & INCLUSION

Our Diversity, Equity & Inclusion (DEI) Mission: Turning Me into We. We have a shared responsibility to create and sustain an enriching and diverse workplace for all APL Logistics colleagues and associates. Anchored to our company Maxims, our DEI strategy is to foster and sustain a company culture where people are respected for who they are, all contributions are valued, and our employees are united to achieve common goals.

CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

8 Ways Shipping & Logistics InMotion is designed to bring attention and aid to the communities in which we live and operate, but also to bring together groups of employees. Cohorts of 3 to 300 colleagues have gathered to plant mangroves, run 5ks, organize book drives, collect plastic from the bottom of the ocean, build libraries, and more.

In 2022, we took the opportunity to revamp our global corporate social responsibility (CSR) program: APLL InMotion. This initiative is dedicated to promoting Accountability, People, Leadership, and Longevity across our global workforce.
